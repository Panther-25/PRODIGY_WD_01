This appears to be the most current and widely discussed entity under this name. It is a Mumbai-based company founded in 2023 that primarily functions as a provider of online, task-based internships for students and beginners in various technology fields.

Prodigy InfoTech operates as an EdTech platform. While the internships themselves are unpaid, the company may generate revenue through other services or partnerships. They position themselves as a stepping stone for students to gain valuable experience for their resumes and future job applications.
